Our Passion..

FLORAL BEAUTY has a big store where we take care of those flowers which are rarely available such as roses of white, yellow, red , pink colors and specially black rose which is very rarely produced and is founded in Rock Garden, India. Customers can also buy tulips, daffodils, sunflowers, jasmine, helicons pray, bird of paradise and lily.

We create a natural floral design with the most beautiful flowers sourced from all over the world. Our main inspiration is Nature with all its forms, colors, textures, and fragrances. Passion for flowers and decades of experience in the floral world allows us to create unique arrangements and bouquets for every special moment, event and interior decoration.

We bring with us over 24 years of experience in the international floral industry, more specifically from India and Canada. Over the years we have had the opportunity to learn from the best and most renowned floral design teachers of India. At our school Floral Design India, thousands of students have learned to become professional florists and decorators.

FLORAL BEAUTY built a mailing list to communicate with its customers and release many advertisements telling customers about new merchandise, special discounts and holiday offers. It is a big expanded business which also provides majority of employment opportunities.
